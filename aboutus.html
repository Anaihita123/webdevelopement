About Us


Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar believes that just information is not education. It is culturing our behavior and attitude. It is our ability to perceive things better. The purpose of education is to increase awareness. Only education that can nourish inbuilt virtues can impart true intelligence. With these thoughts in mind, SRI SRI RAVISHANKAR VIDYA MANDIR TRUST was founded in the year 1999 by Gurudev Sri Sri Ravishankar as the educational wing of the Art of Living, to promote holistic education in a stress-free and child-friendly environment.




Our Vision


SSRVM Najibabad is a school where highest education, cultural and human values imparted to young minds from challenging social and economic background.

Our vision is to transform underprivileged children into healthy and confident adults with high moral and ethical values. We believe that a holistic approach to education is the best way to ensure these children reach their full potential in life.
